Workshop resources

This page hosts the resources and links for the workshop "Digital Humanities for a Medieval World: Public Digital Humanities in the Classroom" at the International Congress on Medieval Studies, May 8, 2025.

Authored by: William H. (Bill) Campbell, Assistant Professor of History and Digital Studies, University of Pittsburgh at Greensburg, whc7@pitt.edu

First steps

  1. Open this page on your own laptop so you can click links directly: https://haggis78.github.io/workshop/icms2025.html
  2. Go to https://kumu.io/ and click the blue "JOIN FOR FREE" button create an account.
  3. Click the green "New Project" button at upper right.

Looking at our data

The source of the data we are using is from the Submission Strategies: The Irish Submissions to Richard II, 1395 project; I adapted material from Dr. Margaret Smith's relations .tsv file to fit the format requirements for Kumu. You can either upload .csv files to Kumu or paste in links to Google Sheets.
