Workshop resources

This page hosts the resources and links for the workshop "Digital Humanities for Teaching: Mapping Viking Travels and Persons in Eirik the Red's Saga" at the Symposium on Medieval and Renaissance Studies at St. Louis University in June 2025.

First steps

  1. Open this page on your own laptop so you can click links directly: https://haggis78.github.io/workshop/
  2. Go to https://kumu.io/ and click the blue "JOIN FOR FREE" button create an account.
  3. Go to https://recogito.pelagios.org/ and click the blue "Register for Recogito" button to create an account.

Quick Mapping with Google My Maps

  1. Google My Maps link: https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/
  2. Sign into your Google account if prompted
  3. Click "Create a New Map"
  4. Add locations:
    • Method A: Search for a location; then click "add to map"
    • Method B: Click on the map to drop a pin; supply a label and, if you want, a link to information
    • Method C: Add a line between two points by clicking; give it a label
